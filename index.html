lifePAD

Primary Artistry Device

What it is

A simple sketch pad that works with touch and stylus input on any device.

What you can do

Draw with different colors and textures, erase mistakes, undo and redo your strokes, and save your artwork as PNG images.

Privacy

Everything stays on your device. No accounts needed. No data sent anywhere. Works completely offline.

Collaboration

How to host:

  1. Enter a session passphrase (shared with joiner)
  2. Click "Create offer" and wait for the offer blob
  3. Copy the offer blob and send it to the joiner (via chat, email, etc.)
  4. Paste the answer blob from the joiner below
  5. Click "Apply answer" to establish connection

How to join:

  1. Enter the same passphrase as the host
  2. Paste the offer blob from the host
  3. Click "Create answer" and wait
  4. Copy the answer blob and send it back to the host
  5. Wait for host to apply answer - connection will establish
